For our video project we looked at some of the rooms around Osburn Hall which is the applied engineering safety and technology building here at Millersville. For the project we took a little tour of what is around the hall. My group made a fun video following two students, while taking a look around the building. We took stops at the woods, metallics, print production, and 3d print labs. We also took a look at the lounge and afew other projects that have been done that are around Osburn.
